About

I was born in Hong Kong and moved to Vancouver, Canada at the ripe old age of 4 years old. Graduating with a degree in Finance, I moved back to Hong Kong shortly after to start a career as an auditor. While being exposed to many different industries during my auditing career, I felt that the technology industry was the most dynamic and where all the innovations was taking place. Technologies including Instagram, virtual reality and the internet of things were beginning to consume and take hold of our lives while I did not understand a thing about the inner workings, which frustrated me as I'm a naturally curious person. Finally, when I was nearing completion of my Certified Public Accountant certification, I decided to take the plunge into programming and havn't looked back since!
